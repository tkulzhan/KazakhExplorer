Welcome to Caspian Riviera Grand Place!

Rixos Water World Aktau is the first and only five-star hotel in Central Asia. The hotel offers its guests a luxurious all inclusive holiday. The resort is located on the picturesque coast of the Caspian Sea near the town of Aktau. Here you can endlessly admire crystal clear azure waters. Indulge yourself in a holiday on the beach with the whole family! The resort, spread out on the purest sandy beach, drowns in the greenery of magnificent gardens, creating the ideal atmosphere for a luxurious holiday. Visit huge pools where you can relax, relax or have fun with the whole family, relax in the sunshine on a special pier, play football or tennis on one of the courts or immerse yourself in the sacrament of relaxation at Rixos Anjana Spa. It is here, in our resort, that the best entertainment in Aktau awaits you! Dip into the holiday with a world-class entertainment program: our fun begins in the morning and does not stop until late at night.
