Welcome to Rixos!

This luxurious 5-star hotel is located in the center of Almaty, 1 km from St. Nicholas Cathedral. Rixos Almaty offers modern rooms with free WiFi and a spa with swimming pool. All Rixos Almaty rooms have air conditioning, a work desk and a balcony with beautiful views. The bathroom has a bath and shower.cA buffet breakfast is served every morning at Rixos Almaty. The restaurant serves international cuisine on the menu, as well as a buffet. The lobby bar and lounge are open 24 hours a day. There is also a Brasserie restaurant. The 2,200 sq.m. spa offers a gym, sauna, Turkish bath, indoor garden and 7 massage rooms. Hotel Rixos is 2 km from Almaty Opera House and 5 km by cable car from Mount Koktobe.
